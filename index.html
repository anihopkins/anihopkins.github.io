ABOUT

Ani Hopkins is a journalist, teacher, and computer scientist. She is working towards obtaining a Bachelor of Science with a focus in cybercommunication in 2019 from Tufts University. Her work is focused on labor journalism, queer and trans issues, and using technology to reach broader audiences with better information.

Ani has written for The Tufts Daily as a staff news writer, a columnist, and a contributing opinion writer. She currently lives in Brooklyn with her boyfriend and teaches for Girls Who Code.

Outside of work, she enjoys comics, cooking, podcasts and trains. She is a member of the New York Trans Boxing Collective and the Tufts University chapter of Women in Computer Science (WiCS).
